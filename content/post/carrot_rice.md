Delicious rice for Easter - pairs great with lamb.
<!--more-->

![Carrot rice](/img/carrot_rice.png "Carrot rice")

**Prep time:** 10 minutes\
**Cook time:** 30 minutes

### Ingredients

* Rice
* Lots of carrots
* Cinnamon

### Directions
1. Boil rice
2. Peel carrots 
3. Chop in cuisinart
4. Add carrots
5. Cook and singe bottom of rice
